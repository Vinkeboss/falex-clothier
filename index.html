What services do you offer?

We offer a variety of services including custom tailoring, ready-to-wear outfits, and alterations. Our goal is to provide high-quality clothing that meets your unique style. Whether you need something casual or formal, we have you covered.

How can I place a custom order?

To place a custom order, simply visit our store or contact us through our website. Our team will guide you through the process and help you choose the perfect fabric and design. We strive to make your vision a reality.

What are your business hours?

Our business hours are Monday to Saturday, from 9 AM to 6 PM. We are closed on Sundays. Feel free to visit us during these hours for any inquiries or fittings.

Do you offer alterations?

Yes, we provide alteration services for all types of clothing. Whether you need adjustments to fit or style, our skilled tailors are here to help. Just bring in your items, and we’ll take care of the rest.
